PACESETTER ISIA

- International Ski Instructors Association -

A pacesetter is someone who is chosen to race and therefore decides the standard run time of the race. After the pacesetter sets this time, all of the ISIA card candidates try to accomplish the run in a previously set percentage time over the pacesetter one, in order to become international fully certified ISIA card instructor.

I've been chosen as an ISIA Pacesetter three times:

  • 2016 Esquel, Chubut, Argentina. Carrera AADIDESS (Asociación Argentina de Instructores de Esquí, Snowboard y Socorristas)
  • 2019 Aspen Highlands, USA. PSIA Race
  • 2019 San Martin de los Andes, Neuquen, Argentina. Carrera AADIDESS
